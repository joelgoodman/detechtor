# Higher Education Technology Categories

This document outlines the comprehensive technology categories we should detect for higher education institutions.

## Core Academic Systems
- **SIS** (Student Information Systems) - Banner, Colleague, PeopleSoft, Jenzabar
- **LMS** (Learning Management Systems) - Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, D2L Brightspace
- **Library Systems** - Ex Libris, OCLC WorldCat, Koha, SirsiDynix, III Sierra
- **Student Success** - Navigate (EAB), Starfish, GradesFirst, Civitas Learning

## Marketing & Communications
- **CRM** (Customer Relationship Management) - Slate, Salesforce, HubSpot, Radius
- **CMS** (Content Management Systems) - Drupal, WordPress, OmniCMS, TerminalFour
- **DAM** (Digital Asset Management) - Bynder, Widen, Adobe Experience Manager, Brandfolder
- **Marketing Automation** - Marketo, Pardot, Mailchimp, Constant Contact
- **Social Media Management** - Hootsuite, Buffer, Sprout Social

## Infrastructure & Technical
- **CDN** (Content Delivery Networks) - Cloudflare, AWS CloudFront, Akamai, KeyCDN, Fastly
- **Web Servers** - Apache, Nginx, IIS, LiteSpeed
- **Hosting Providers** - AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, Pantheon, Acquia
- **SSL/Security** - Let's Encrypt, DigiCert, Comodo, CloudFlare SSL
- **Load Balancers** - F5, HAProxy, AWS ELB, Cloudflare Load Balancing
- **Monitoring** - New Relic, Datadog, Pingdom, Google Analytics

## Authentication & Security
- **SSO/Identity** - Shibboleth, CAS, SAML, Active Directory, Okta, Auth0
- **Multi-Factor Authentication** - Duo Security, RSA SecurID, Google Authenticator
- **Password Management** - LastPass, 1Password, Bitwarden (institutional)

## Financial & Administrative
- **ERP** (Enterprise Resource Planning) - Oracle, SAP, Workday, Infor
- **Financial Aid** - PowerFAIDS, CollegeSource, CampusLogic, ScholarshipUniverse
- **HR Systems** - Workday, BambooHR, ADP, Paycom
- **Procurement** - Jaggaer, Ariba, Coupa

## Analytics & Data
- **Web Analytics** - Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, Hotjar, Crazy Egg
- **Business Intelligence** - Tableau, Power BI, Qlik, Looker
- **Data Warehousing** - Snowflake, AWS Redshift, Google BigQuery
- **Survey Tools** - Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey, Typeform

## Events & Campus Life
- **Event Management** - Eventbrite, EMS, 25Live, CollegiateLink
- **Calendar Systems** - Google Calendar, Outlook, Localist
- **Booking Systems** - LibCal, BookitLive, Acuity Scheduling

## Communication & Collaboration
- **Email Systems** - Gmail, Outlook 365, Exchange
- **Video Conferencing** - Zoom, Microsoft Teams, WebEx, Google Meet
- **Chat/Messaging** - Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord
- **File Sharing** - Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, Box

## Alumni & Fundraising
- **Alumni Relations** - Blackbaud, iModules, Graduway
- **Fundraising** - Blackbaud Raiser's Edge, DonorPerfect, Salesforce NPSP
- **Event Fundraising** - Greater Giving, BiddingOwl, CharityAuctionsToday

## Media & Creative
- **Video Platforms** - Kaltura, Panopto, Vimeo, YouTube, Wistia
- **Image/Photo Management** - Adobe Creative Cloud, Canva, Figma
- **Digital Signage** - Four Winds, Scala, BrightSign

## Mobile & Apps
- **Mobile App Platforms** - Rave, Campus Labs, Ready Education
- **Push Notifications** - Firebase, OneSignal, Urban Airship
- **Mobile Payment** - TouchNet, Blackboard Transact

## Accessibility & Compliance
- **Accessibility Tools** - AccessiBe, UserWay, AudioEye, WAVE
- **Compliance** - OneTrust, TrustArc (privacy compliance)
- **ADA Tools** - Deque axe, Level Access

## Research & Academic Tools
- **Research Management** - InfoEd, Kuali Research, ERA
- **Plagiarism Detection** - Turnitin, SafeAssign, Grammarly
- **Citation Management** - EndNote, Zotero, Mendeley

## Specialized Higher Ed
- **Admissions** - Common Application, ApplyTexas, Coalition Application
- **Transcript Services** - National Student Clearinghouse, Parchment
- **Course Catalogs** - Acalog, CourseLeaf, SmartCatalog
- **Scheduling** - Ad Astra, Infosilem, CollegeNET
- **Campus Safety** - Rave Guardian, LiveSafe, AppArmor

## E-commerce & Payments
- **Payment Processing** - TouchNet, Stripe, PayPal, Square
- **Bookstore** - Follett, Barnes & Noble Education, VitalSource
- **Meal Plans** - CBORD, Blackboard Transact

## Testing & Assessment
- **Online Proctoring** - ProctorU, Honorlock, Respondus
- **Assessment Tools** - Pearson MyLab, McGraw-Hill Connect
- **Testing Centers** - PSI Services, Pearson VUE

## Communication Platforms
- **Emergency Alerts** - Rave Mobile Safety, Everbridge, AlertMedia
- **Digital Displays** - Rise Vision, ScreenCloud, NoviSign
- **Wayfinding** - Concept3D, MapYourTag, Jibestream

This comprehensive list ensures we capture the full technology ecosystem that higher education institutions use, from core academic systems to specialized tools and infrastructure components.
