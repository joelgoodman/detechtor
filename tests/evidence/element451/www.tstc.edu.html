New Students

Choose from one of our technical programs like electrical systems, precision machining, or cybersecurity. Our expert instructors and advanced labs train you to solve problems and lead projects. We start where you’re at and get you job-ready.
Take the First Step

Career-Changers

Shift gears with our focused programs in fields like instrumentation or medical equipment repair. Build on your existing experience with a certificate or degree or use your experience to move ahead in classes and take advantage of flexible scheduling.
Change Your Career

Veterans

Your military experience is valuable, and we can help you turn it into a powerful civilian career. Use your VA benefits, get credit for your military training, and choose programs that build on your knowledge and experience. We’re here to honor your service by preparing you for the next step in life.
Support for Veterans

Employers

Build your workforce with skilled technicians trained in Automation, Advanced Manufacturing, Cybersecurity, and other high-demand fields. TSTC graduates are job-ready, reliable, and prepared to make an impact from day one. Partner with us to fill critical roles and strengthen your company’s future.
Hire TSTC Grads

Business Leaders

Keep your team skilled, your operations efficient, and your business competitive when you invest in continued training.
